Zaun

On the fictional world of Runeterra (translates to magical earth) is located the city-state of Zaun. It is a large, undercity district, lying in the deep canyons and valleys threading Piltover, another city-state. The creation of Zaun was inspired by Dieselpunk, Biopunk and Steampunk fantasy genres.

Zaun is a part of the lore of League of Legends
